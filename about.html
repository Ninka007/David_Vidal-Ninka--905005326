About Us

Football Agency Sierra Leone is dedicated to nurturing football talent across Sierra Leone and connecting it with global opportunities. Established in 2010, we've grown to become a leading agency known for our commitment to player development, ethical representation, and strategic partnerships.

2010: Founding of the Agency

Football Agency Sierra Leone was established with a vision to scout and develop local talent, providing them a pathway to professional careers.

2015: First International Transfer

Achieved our first major international transfer, marking a significant milestone in connecting Sierra Leonean players with European clubs.

Our Vision

  • To be the premier football agency in West Africa, recognized for integrity.
  • To foster a new generation of Sierra Leonean football stars who inspire their communities.

Our Mission

  • Identify, develop, and professionally manage top football talent in Sierra Leone.
  • Provide comprehensive support services, including career guidance and financial literacy.
  • Facilitate strategic placements in leading leagues worldwide.
